Collaborative Robot with Electromagnetic Gripper for Metallic Object Handling

The collaborative robot with an electromagnetic gripper represents a significant advancement in robotic manipulation capabilities, particularly in environments where metallic objects are prevalent.

As part of the electrical team, we were responsible for selecting the appropriate motors and calculating the required torque for efficient operation of the robot arm and gripper assembly. Through rigorous analysis and testing, we ensured optimal performance and reliability.

Robot Vacuum Cleaner Using SLAM and ROS

The robot vacuum cleaner was equipped with various sensors including LIDAR (Light Detection and Ranging) and wheel encoders for accurate localization and navigation. Additionally, it featured a powerful suction system and a dustbin for efficient cleaning.I employed state-of-the-art SLAM algorithms such as Hector SLAM and Google Cartographer to enable the robot to build maps of its environment and simultaneously determine its location within those maps.
